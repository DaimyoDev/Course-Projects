Robusta Coffee Beans

Robusta Coffee Beans produced in Sub-Saharan Africa, their taste is woody or nutty with a bitter taste to it.

Excelsa Coffee Beans

Excelsa Coffee Beans are a unique type of Coffee bean with a unique complex flavor. Grown in Southeast Asia.

Arabica Coffee Beans

A somewhat variable taste. It is in the middle of bitterness. Arabica is despite the name, unique to Ethiopia.

Liberica Coffee Beans

Similar to the taste of Robusta Coffee Beans but different. also grown in Southeast Asia.

Mocha

Mocha is a type of coffee that is really more of a chocolate milkshake with coffee mixed in with it. There is an option to have whip cream or not.

Americano

Invented in WW2 it is a coffee that has a high amount of water mixed in with the coffee.

Macchiato

A simple coffee with steamed milk and an espresso shot of coffee.

Cappuccino

A complex mixture of coffee, steamed and foamed milk, and chocolate. A favorite morning coffee in Italy.
